Why TeeUP exists

This is where you share your story — feeling lost when you started golf, and why you want to make it easier for other families.

“When I started golf, I was completely lost.”

I didn’t grow up with golf lessons or a built-in coach. When I first tried to get into the game, I was confused, intimidated, and honestly had no idea what I was doing.

As I kept learning, I noticed how many kids — and their parents — felt the same way. Especially as an assistant coach volunteer, I saw kids struggling with gear that didn’t fit, bags that were too heavy, and pressure that sucked the fun out of it.

TeeUP is my way of sharing everything I wish someone had told me at the beginning: how to start small, keep it fun, and focus on the parts of golf that help kids grow.

My promise: I’ll always explain things the way I’d explain them to a friend — no snobbery, no confusing jargon, and no recommendations I wouldn’t give to the kids I coach.
